# Claire's Photo Gallery

## How to Add Photos:

1. **Drop your photos here** - Simply copy and paste your romantic photos into this folder
2. **Name them correctly** - Use the naming convention below
3. **Refresh the website** - Your photos will appear automatically

## Required Photo Names:
- `photo1.jpg` - Your first special moment
- `photo2.jpg` - Forever together
- `photo3.jpg` - Love across distance
- `photo4.jpg` - My everything
- `photo5.jpg` - Beautiful memories
- `photo6.jpg` - Always in my heart
- `photo7.jpg` - Perfect moment
- `photo8.jpg` - My sunshine

## Photo Guidelines:
- **Recommended size:** 800x800 pixels (square)
- **File size:** Under 500KB each for fast loading
- **Format:** JPG or PNG
- **Quality:** High-quality romantic photos

## Tips:
- Use photos that capture special moments together
- Include a mix of close-ups and scenic photos
- Ensure good lighting and composition
- Consider the romantic theme when selecting photos

## Supported Formats:
- `.jpg` - Recommended
- `.jpeg` - Also supported
- `.png` - Supported but larger file size

## Example:
Just drag and drop your photos here:
```
/public/gallery/
├── photo1.jpg
├── photo2.jpg
├── photo3.jpg
└── ... (your photos)
```

The gallery will automatically update when you refresh the website! 🎉
